## About the Site üöÄ

---

Blockchain-based eCommerce warranty system using NFTs
The objective is to replace the physical warranty and have block chain based warranty using NFT which will ensure
authenticity and security.

<br>

‚óè Converting ownership authenticity and product warranty cards into decaying NFTs.<br>
‚óè For instance, allow brands and retailers to introduce an NFT for each of their products, which allows
customers to receive the physical product along with a digital version of it.<br>
‚óè Customers can then use the digital NFT to verify the authenticity of their product, prove their ownership of their product, and transfer ownership of them upon resale.<br>
‚óè The brand/retailer should also be able to tie the digital NFT to its warranty program, allowing owners to trackrepairs and replacements to the original item.<br>
‚óè Decay the NFT once the warranty is over.<br>
‚óè WE used the Polygon blockchain to deploy our solution and demo the final product as a web prototype<br>
